Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to contact for course information?

+

For quick course information, calling us directly or messaging on WhatsApp is fastest. For detailed syllabus and curriculum, email is preferred.

Do you offer online classes?

+

Yes, we offer both in-person and online classes for most courses. Contact us to learn about our online learning options and requirements.

Can I visit the College before enrolling?

+

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to visit our campus, meet our instructors, and see our facilities. You can even attend a demo class.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, bank transfers, and mobile payments (EasyPaisa/JazzCash). Installment plans are also available for most courses.

Do you provide certificates after course completion?

+

Yes, all students receive a recognized certificate upon successful completion of their course, which can help with job applications and career advancement.
